Project Objective & Results

Redesign the brand identity for East Side Games, developing a brand book that includes a new logotype, marketing assets, and the ideation process.

Challenges and Takeaways

This project was an opportunity to explore branding and logo design. My design choices, including the bold typography, vibrant monochrome color palette, were driven by the need to reflect East Side Games’ products and company values. The creative process involved ideation and continuous iteration based on feedback, which helped refine the final outcome.

One of the main challenges was selecting a color palette that can be incorporated into the logo and marketing assets, and I approached this by testing the design with various color palettes.

This project deepened my understanding of creating a brand identity, and while I’m proud of achieving a cohesive visual language, I see room for improvement in optimizing the layout for different print and digital mediums.
